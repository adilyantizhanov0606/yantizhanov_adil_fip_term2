Our Story

“At ZIMA, we believe true premium quality begins with purity. Inspired by the meaning of the word “Zima” — winter — our brand was born from the idea of capturing the calm strength, clarity, and timeless elegance of the coldest season. Winter represents stillness, confidence, and precision, and these values continue to shape everything we create.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to craft a vodka experience that feels smooth, elevated, and unforgettable. From the very first bottle, ZIMA Elite was designed to embody refined taste, exceptional balance, and a sense of quiet prestige.

Each variety in our collection offers its own character while staying true to the essence of the brand. The original expression delivers crystal-clear smoothness and timeless purity. ZIMA Grape introduces a richer, velvety depth with subtle fruit warmth, while ZIMA Lemon brings bright citrus freshness and a crisp, energetic finish.

More than a beverage, ZIMA Elite is created for moments that deserve to be remembered — celebrations, connections, and the confidence of choosing something exceptional. Our philosophy is rooted in sophistication, authenticity, and the belief that luxury should feel effortless.”

- Founder & CEO
